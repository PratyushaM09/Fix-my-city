Frequently asked questions

Is Fixly free for residents? +

Yes, reporting and tracking issues is completely free for residents, students, and community members.

Can I report an issue anonymously? +

Absolutely. Toggle Anonymous Reporting on the report form to hide your identity from the public feed.

How does AI duplicate detection work? +

Fixly compares location, category, and image similarity to merge duplicate reports automatically, reducing noise for departments.

Can my college campus or society join? +

Yes — Fixly supports neighborhoods, residential societies, and college campuses with dedicated community spaces.
